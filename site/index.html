Our Story

The human behind the course.

Kenzo
Founder, Crypto Basics

I'm not a financial advisor. I'm not a crypto expert. I'm just someone who spent hundreds of hours watching YouTube videos about crypto — and still couldn't explain it to my sister.

That frustrated me more than I expected. Because I knew I was onto something. I could feel it. But every time I tried to share it, I got blank stares. I felt like a mute talking about the most exciting thing I'd ever discovered.

And here's the thing — smart, educated, self-aware people are getting taken advantage of every day in this space. Not because they're foolish. Because nobody taught them the basics. Scammers love that gap. I refuse to leave it open.

We are living through a financial revolution. Crypto. AI. Tokenization. It's moving faster than the internet did. Faster than anything in history. And most people don't even have the vocabulary to follow the conversation yet.

"I built what I wish someone had built for me."
